Houses or Museums?

Lina Bo Bardi, 1958

What should come first, houses or museums? Everything at once: the houses, the schools, the museums, the libraries. Urban planning cannot ignore cultural issues.

Museum? What is a museum? In everyday life, when we want to describe a person, thing or idea that is outdated or useless, we often say “they belong in a museum.”

The modern museum has to be a didactic museum, able to marry conservation with the message that it is the art that must be highlighted.

First published in Diário de Notícias (Salvador, Bahia), 5 October 1958
